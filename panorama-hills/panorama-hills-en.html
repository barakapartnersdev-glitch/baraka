The Project

Land with a view, and a clear path to value

Panorama Hills is a residential land development set on an elevated green hillside in one of Cyprus's most scenic coastal regions. The site has been planned for division into serviced residential building plots, each positioned to capture panoramic views over the bay and the surrounding countryside. With its development permit and registered title already in place, the project offers a rare combination of a premier location and a de-risked legal foundation.
